Industry-Recognized Training

Good Morning Electrical Engineering Academy is a leading vocational and technical training institution in Ghana, dedicated to equipping students with the practical skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the electrical engineering industry.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about admissions and training at our academy.

What are the admission requirements?

Applicants should have completed basic education (JHS or equivalent). No prior electrical experience is required for entry-level programmes. Bring a valid ID, passport photos, and educational certificates when applying.

How long are the training programmes?

Programme durations range from 8 to 20 months depending on the course. Electrical Engineering Technology (Cert II) runs 12–18 months, Marine Electrical Engineering 10–14 months, Industrial Installation 14–20 months, and Electrical Monitoring Rewinding 8–12 months.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible installment payment plans to make quality electrical training accessible. Contact our admissions office to discuss payment options that work for your situation.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

Yes. Upon successful completion of your programme, you will receive a nationally recognized certificate demonstrating your competence in your chosen electrical engineering specialization.

Can I visit the academy before enrolling?

Absolutely! We welcome prospective students and parents to visit our campus, tour our workshops, and meet our instructors. Walk-ins are welcome during business hours, or call ahead to schedule a guided tour.

What career support do you provide after graduation?

We provide career guidance, entrepreneurship support, and connect graduates with employment opportunities in the electrical industry. Many of our alumni have gone on to successful careers as technicians, contractors, and specialists.
